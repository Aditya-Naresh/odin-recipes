Sardine Fry

Sardines are small epipelagic fish that sometimes migrate along the coast in large schools. They are an important forage fish for larger forms of marine life.

Ingredients

  • Sardines
  • salt
  • Chilli powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Minced Garlic
  • Minced Ginger
  • Vinegar
  • Fresh Curry Leaves
  • Coconut oil

Cooking

  1. Wash the cleaned, scored fish.Rub with salt - leave a couple of minutes and wash off
  2. Marinate the fish with all the ingredients
  3. Heat the coconut oil in the frying pan
  4. Add and fry the fish side by side
